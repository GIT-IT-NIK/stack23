#

Demystifying AI/ML

Abstract

What was once unknown can now be quickly discovered with a few queries. Decision makers no longer have to rely on gut instinct. With AIML frameworks, they have more extensive and precise evidence at their fingertips.
New sources of data, fed into systems powered by machine learning and AI, are at the heart of this transformation. The information flowing through the physical world and the global economy is staggering in scope. It comes from thousands of sources: sensors, satellite imagery, web traffic, digital apps, videos, and credit card transactions, just to name a few. These types of data sets are the next evolution in decision making. AI is not only finding answers faster but creating transparency around issues that have always been murky.
Data science teams can build models with miraculous capabilities, but it’s unlikely that they can solve highly specific business problems on their own. Hence AIML frameworks need to aligned with industry outcomes, else they risk becoming inefficient. And that is the precise challenge facing many industries – from Health care to Retail, from Logistics to Transportation, from Service Providers to Enterprises.
This workshop will deep dive into how industries are harnessing the power of AI/ML frameworks for solving complex business challenges and creating new service models. It will empower you with an industry peek into how AI/ML works and how it does not.
