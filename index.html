about us

STACK, standing for Science Technology And Computing Knowledge, brings forth an amalgamation of both technical and non technical events, also known as TRACKS. And in addition to the unique events, it creates a platform for students to interact with eminent speakers from various leading industries.

These TRACKS are very endearing and enriching events that will specialise in preparing students to face real world challenges. Featuring Technical/Non technical Talks, Competitions , Case studies, internships and project opportunities, STACK is reaching out to students all across the nation.
